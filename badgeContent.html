Artwork Enhancement

Our in-house designers can re-draw your low resolution images into superb high resolution artwork. We can even develop an image from scratch using just your description or hand-drawn sketch.

Your artwork can be blurred, uneven or poorly coloured, yet we can quickly and efficiently enhance it to ensure your brand and logo meets the quality standards you desire.

Vector Image Benefits:

  • Print at any resolution without quality loss
  • Scale up and down infinitely while maintaining sharpness
  • Quickly change colors without affecting quality

Accepted Formats:

JPEG PNG PDF EPS AI PSD

Embroidery Digitizing

Whatever your logo, we can create it as an embroidery. All we require is a good standard piece of artwork to work from.

Important Note:

With embroidery we create logos with physical properties (thread). The smaller the logo, the less clear fine details or text will appear.
