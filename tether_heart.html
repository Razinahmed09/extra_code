Introduction

TetherHeart is where emotional intelligence meets secure connection. We build tools to help couples deepen their bond - and your privacy is the foundation of that trust.
This Privacy Policy explains how TetherHeart LLC collects, uses, protects, and shares your data when you use our smart rings, mobile app, and website

What We Collect

Personal Identifiers:
  1. - Name, email address, and relationship profile info
  2. - Optional fields like anniversaries, preferences, or religion
Biometric & Emotional Data:
  1. - Heart rate and variability (HRV)
  2. - Skin temperature (where supported)
  3. - Emotional tagging via Green/Yellow/Red inputs
  4. - Ring interactions, heartbeat sharing, and mood-based responses
Device & Usage Data:
  1. - Device type, operating system, and app version
  2. - Bluetooth pairing logs
  3. - Session data, crash reports, and engagement metrics
Optional Location Data:
  1. - Only collected with explicit permission

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
  1. - Sync real-time biometric data between partners
  2. - Display emotional trends and personalized reflections
  3. - Deliver guidance through TetherHeart-GPT
  4. - Improve product performance and emotional insight quality
  5. - Support anonymized research and wellness technology development

Anonymous Data Sharing & Opt-Out

TetherHeart LLC may use aggregated, anonymized data to:
  1. - Enhance our AI emotional framework
  2. - Support relationship wellness research
  3. - Train and refine algorithms
  4. - Engage in partnerships with academic or commercial partners
You may opt out of anonymous data use at any time by:
  1. - Disabling the option in Settings -> Privacy in the app
  2. - Or emailing privacy@tetherheart.com with the subject "Opt-Out of Data Sharing"
Your core experience will remain unaffected.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell personal information (name, email, identifiable biometric data).
We only share data with:
  • - Secure cloud services under strict data agreements
  • - Analytics providers with anonymization guarantees
  • - Legal authorities when required by applicable law
    • We follow the privacy frameworks of GDPR, CCPA, and similar global standards.

Data Security

Your data is safeguarded using:
  • - End-to-end encryption between app and ring
  • - Role-based access and PAM (Privileged Access Management)
  • - Network, cloud, mobile, and IoT security layers
  • - Secure device pairing and unlinking protocols
We continually review and enhance our security systems.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your region, you have the right to:
  • - Access your stored data
  • - Correct or update personal info
  • - Request deletion of your account and data
  • - Opt out of any data sale or sharing
  • - Lodge complaints with your data protection authority
To exercise your rights, contact: privacy@tetherheart.com

Children's Data

TetherHeart is intended for users aged 13 or older. We do not knowingly collect or process data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our services or legal requirements. You'll be notified via email or in-app messaging when updates are significant.

Smart Ring Compatibility & Exclusivity

TetherHeart smart rings are exclusively designed to pair with each other - forming a secure, one-to-one emotional and biometric connection.
  • - Rings cannot be paired with third-party smart rings (e.g., Oura, Whoop, Ultrahuman)
  • - If unlinked, a ring becomes a "solo" mode device and cannot re-pair unless reset
  • - This pairing system is part of our emotional fidelity and privacy-first design philosophy

    • We do not support or guarantee functionality with third-party devices. Attempting external pairing may result in limited or unsupported behavior.
